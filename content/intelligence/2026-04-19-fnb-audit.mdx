FNB listed **847 tech roles** across LinkedIn and PNet in Q1 2026 — down from **1,101 in Q4 2025**. That's a 31% contraction in a single quarter, and it's not a pipeline delay.

Cross-referencing with TymeBank (+44%) and Yoco (+31%) over the same window tells the real story. The talent is moving — it's just not moving toward Johannesburg CBD anymore.

**42 FNB roles** have been open for 60+ days with no update. That's not a talent shortage. That's a budget freeze with a careers page still running.
